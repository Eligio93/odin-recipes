Recipe Pasta Tonno

Description

This canned tuna pasta recipe is quick, healthy, and comforting. The perfect recipe when you don't have a lot in your pantry!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Boil a salted pot of water
  2. When the pasta is close to being ready, add the oil to a small pan over medium heat
  3. Stir in the tuna, lemon juice, and parsley
  4. Cook the pasta
  5. Once the pasta is done season with salt & pepper as needed.
